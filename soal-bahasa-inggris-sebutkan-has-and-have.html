14. Apakah perbedaan has dan have dalam bahasa inggris.!JELASKAN


penggunaan has untuk he, she, and it sedangkan have untuk I, you, we and they...

   Have digunakan pada simple present tense dan digunakan untuk mengikuti subjek seperti (I, you, we, they dan subjek lebih dari satu orang seperti The students). Contoh: -   I have a lot of friends


 Has juga digunakan pada simple present tense, namun khusus mengikuti subjek seperti (she, he, it,  orang tunggal seperti the student). Contoh: -  She has a lot of boyfriends

15. Penggunaan Has Have and Had


I, She, He, It utk tobe Has
You, We, They utk tobe Have
Had digunakan utk Verb3
Has dan Have : Verb1 dan 2


Contoh :
He has a pen
You have a new car
The class had 40 students
Has => digunakan untuk subject " SHE, HE, IT "
Have => digunakan untuk subject " THEY, WE, I, YOU "
Had => digunakan untuk semua subject

Bedanya has, have, sama had :
Has / Have = bentuk PRESENT
Had = bentuk PAST

16. Pengggunaan "had have" dalam bahasa inggris


----Jawaban----
Had = Dipakai dalam past perfect tense, berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan hal yang telah dilakukan dan telah berhenti
Contoh = I had told him to stop cheating. (Untuk past perfect)

Have = Bisa untuk mengungkapkan sebuah kepemilikan atau dipakai dalam present perfect tense, berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan hal yang telah dilakukan dan masih berlanjut hingga sekarang 
Contoh =
- I have many pencils. (Untuk mengungkapkan kepemilikan) 
- I have walked for so long since I went out from my apartment. (Untuk present perfect)

22. What is the difference of had,has,and have?

Has' is the third person singular present tense of 'have' while 'had' is the third person singular past tense and past participle of 'have. ... Both are transitive verbs, but 'has' is used in sentences that talk about the present while 'had' is used in sentences that talk about the past
