13. Soal!!Deskripsikan serigala,penyu,burung,dan dua kuda dalam bahasa Inggris!Rules:• Jangan ngasal• Jangan liat google• Jangan ambil poin doang​


Jawaban:

Serigala (Wolf):

The wolf is a wild carnivorous mammal belonging to the Canidae family. It is known for its strong social bonds and its ability to hunt in packs. Wolves have a distinct appearance with a bushy tail, sharp teeth, and a thick fur coat. They are known for their howling communication and their remarkable hunting skills.

Penyu (Turtle):

The turtle is a reptile that belongs to the Testudines order. It is characterized by its protective shell, which acts as its home and defense mechanism. Turtles have a streamlined body, flippers for swimming, and a beak-like mouth. They are known for their longevity and their ability to retract their head and limbs into their shell for protection.

Burung (Bird):

Birds are warm-blooded vertebrates characterized by their feathers, beaks, and ability to fly. They come in various shapes, sizes, and colors. Birds have wings that enable them to soar through the sky and build nests for their offspring. They communicate through a variety of sounds and songs. Birds play an essential role in pollination, seed dispersal, and maintaining the ecosystem's balance.

Kuda (Horse):

Horses are large, domesticated mammals known for their strength, speed, and endurance. They have well-developed body with strong legs, hooves, and a long mane. Horses are herbivores and have been used by humans for transportation, agriculture, and various recreational activities such as horseback riding and racing. They have a remarkable bond with humans and have played a significant role throughout history.

Dua Kuda (Two Horses):

The two horses are beautiful animals with muscular bodies and sleek coats. They stand side by side, displaying their elegance and strength. Their manes flow in the wind as they graze peacefully in the pasture. These horses are companions, often seen together, and their synchronized movements showcase their harmony and synchrony.
