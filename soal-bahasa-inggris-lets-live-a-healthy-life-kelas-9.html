Jawaban:

Body size doesn't define a person as healthy or sick. Not every skinny people are healthy and not every fleshy people are not healthy. Healthy food makes people healthy. What are healthy foods?

Healthy food is not always about fruits and vegetables. But, it's about how we can make our daily needs balanced. For a day, human needs 60% of carbohydrate, 20% of fats, and 20% of protein.

Carbohydrates are rice, potatoe, corn, green vegetables, etc. Fats are fish, avocado, seeds, etc. While proteins are chicken, beef, Yoghurt, etc.

And to keep them balance, we actually need to calculate them all, calories and also the nutritions. That's why we need the help of some apps at google store or app store. For examples are myfitnesspal and fatsecret.

34. |\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\|\9 × 11 =4 × 11 = Note ; Life and Live​


Jawaban9 × 11 = 994 × 11 = 44===============================

Perkalian merupakan bentuk nilai (angka) yang berulang. Sesuai yang ditanyakan. Misalnya ;

12 × 2 = .. ? Artinya, 12 tersebut 2 kali dikalikan.

===============================Pembahasan

Versi pertama ada dilampiran

Versi kedua

9 × 11

= 9 + 9 + 9 + 9 + 9 + 9 + 9 + 9 + 9 + 9 + 9

= 99 ✔️

4 × 11

= 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 + 4

= 44 ✔️

Mapel : Matematika

Kode kategorisasi : 2.2.3

____________

» Penyelesaian

Nomor 1

→ 9 × 11

→ 9 × (10 + 1)

→ (9 × 10) + (9 × 1)

→ 90 + 9

→ 99

Nomor 2

→ 4 × 11

→ 4 × (10 + 1)

→ (4 × 10) + (4 × 1)

→ 40 + 4

→ 44
