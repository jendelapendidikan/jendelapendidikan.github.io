4. Buat kan cerita libur simple past tense dengan bahasa inggris dan letak simple past tense ​


Jawaban:

Once upon a time, during the summer holiday, my family and I decided to go on a memorable vacation. We traveled to a beautiful island with white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters.

On the first day of our vacation, we arrived at the island and checked into a cozy beachfront resort. After settling in, we went for a long walk along the shoreline and collected seashells as souvenirs.

The next morning, we woke up early and went snorkeling in the vibrant coral reefs. We saw colorful fish and even swam with a playful dolphin. It was an incredible experience that we would cherish forever.

In the afternoon, we rented bicycles and explored the island's lush forests and picturesque villages. We stopped by a local market and bought some delicious tropical fruits.

On the third day, we decided to try surfing. It was challenging, but we had a lot of fun riding the waves and laughing at each other's wipeouts.

During the evenings, we enjoyed the stunning sunset from the resort's veranda. We played card games and shared stories around the bonfire on the beach.

On the fourth day, we took a boat tour to a nearby uninhabited island. The sandy beach was secluded, and we had a picnic surrounded by nature's beauty.

As the week went by, we also visited a historic lighthouse and learned about the island's rich heritage.

On our last day, we took a relaxing boat cruise to see dolphins and whales in their natural habitat. It was an awe-inspiring sight, and we took many photos to remember the moment.

Sadly, the vacation came to an end, and we had to say goodbye to the beautiful island. We packed our bags and boarded the flight back home.

The holiday was filled with laughter, joy, and precious moments spent with loved ones. It was undoubtedly the best vacation we had ever experienced.

21. Buatlah cerita tentang HUT RI menggunakan bahasa inggris simple past tense


Cerita Hari Ulang Tahun Republik Indonesia dengan menggunakan bahasa Inggris dalam bentuk simple past tense:

Yesterday I celebrated Indonesian's Independence Day with my friends in our neighborhood. It was a massive celebration. We held many competitions for the day. Many kids from next block joined our competition. I myself participated in table tennis competition. I got the second place, though. There were Bakiak race, Karung race, Kelereng race, Kurupuk eating competition, and the most awaited competition: Pinang climbing competition. Pinang climbing is Indonesian tradition in celebrating independence day. In that competition, there were many teams, climbing a tall bamboo which held many things on it's peak. But the bamboo was covered by oil, like a lot of oil, so it was slippery. Each teams struggle to reach the peak. It was so fun and my favorite moment.

Pembahasan

Teks cerita di atas dibuat dengan menggunakan simple past tense karena menceritakan pengalaman. Yang namanya pengalaman berarti sudah terjadi di masa lampau, yang dalam cerita di atas adalah yesterday atau kemarin. Simple past tense yang kita gunakan sangat mudah, memakai verb 2 dalam bentuk regular dan irregular.

33. cerita masa lampau menggunakan past tense dalam bahasa inggris


Once upon a time, in a land far far away, there lived a little witch. This witch wasn't like any other witch, for she had beautiful features, and seducing magic.

One day, the witch was taking a stroll around town when her cap flew off. She chased after it, just to find a prince had caught it.
She thanked the prince and left. When she left, the prince thought, "What a lovely lady! She must be from the country! And what is this feeling.....?" He thought about the girl for days, and weeks, thinking about how he could meet him again.

2 weeks after the encounter, the witch decided to go to the town's market to buy some food. There, she met the prince. She called out to him and offered him something. The prince, knowing he was in love with her, took it without hesitation and thanked her. Then they both took each others hands and walked together.

But, unknowing to them, they were being followed. From a dark alleyway, came out a green haired priest. The priest have actually confessed to the prince thousands of times, but was always rejected. So, feeling jealous, she researched more about the witch.

A week later, the priest found out that the girl was a witch with seducing magic. She then took out a search warrant for the witch. Soon after, the news about the witch came to the royal palace and the prince demanded an explanation from both the witch, aka his lover, and the priest, aka his childhood friend.

After they explained everything, the prince decided to burn the witch. The priest agreed, and smiled triumphantly. After 2 days, she decided that that day will be the excecution day.

The witch was taken out from her jail cell and beaten up. When she got outside, she met the prince, and the priest. The witch told the prince "If you think that our love is black magic, then I shall repent." The priest didn't had time for them, and commanded her guards to crucifix her. The prince then went closer to her, and took her long, silky hair and cut if off. He then said "Repent all you want. I'll never forgive you."

When the crucifix was up, the witch felt all sorts of emotions. Love, hatred, Loss, etc. but she felt Rage, Hatred and Betrayal more than anything. She then recited a spell and black wings appeared from her back. She then flew away with tears in her eyes.
