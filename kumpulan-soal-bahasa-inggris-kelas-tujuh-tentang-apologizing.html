18. berikan contoh 10 apologizing dan response dalam bahasa inggris !​


JAWABAN :

Apologizing :

- I'm sorry ( Aku minta maaf )

- I'm so sorry ( Aku sangat minta maaf )

- I'm terribbly sorry ( Aku sangat-sangat minta maaf )

- I'm really sorry ( Aku benar benar minta maaf )

- Pardon me for.. ( Maafkan aku atas.. )

- Sorry,please don't be mad at me ( Maaf,tolong jangan marah padaku )

- I'm sorry,I shouldn't have do that ( Maafkan aku,seharusnya aku tidak melakukan itu )

- I do apologize for.. ( Aku meminta maaf untuk.. )

- Please,accept my apology ( Tolong terima permintaan maafku )

- Sorry,it was my fault ( Maaf itu semua salahku )

Respons :

- It's fine / It's okay ( Tidak apa-apa )

- That's fine ( Bukan apa-apa )

- Forget it ( Lupakan saja )

- Don't worry about it ( Jangan khawatir tentang itu )

- It doesn't matter ( Itu bukan masalah )

- Don't mention it ( Jangan sebutkan itu )

- Don't apologize ( Jangan meminta maaf )

- Never Mind ( Jangan dipikirkan )

Jawaban:

Nisa: Good afternoon, Aura. What are you doing?

Aura: Good afternoon, Nisa. I'm waiting for my friend, Edo.

Nisa: Who's Edo? How come I've never seen him around before?

Aura: He is the new student in our class.

Nisa: Ooh, how come you know him already?

Aura: He's been my bestfriend since childhood, but he moved to Bandung a few years ago. I'm very happy he's going back to Jakarta. Oh, there he is. Hey Edo!

*Edo arrives*

Edo: Hey, Aura! It's so good to see you again.

Aura: Edo, I want you to meet my friend Nisa. And Nisa, this is Edo.

Edo: Hello, Nisa. Nice to meet you.

Nisa: Nice to meet you too, Edo. Welcome to our school.

Edo: Thank you. Can we go to our class now? I want to know where I sit.

Aura: Of course. Come on, Edo and Nisa!

Parting

Rakha: Thanks for helping me clean the classroom, Andi.

Andi: No problem, Rakha.

Rakha: It really bothers me that the others don't help clean our classroom. It only takes a few minutes to clean.

Andi: I know, right? And if the classroom gets dirty, they are the ones who are going to get uncomfortable while studying.

Rakha: Yeah. I will tell them to help us clean next time.

Andi: Good idea. If they disagree, we should report to our teacher and tell that the others do not want to clean the class.

Rakha: Yup, I bet they will do it if the teacher told them to.

Andi: By the way, the classroom is clean. I have to go now. My mom is waiting for me.

Rakha: Okay, Andi. See you tomorrow.

Andi: See you tomorrow.

Thanking

Bunga: Hey, sis. Look what I made for you to wear to your birthday party tomorrow.

Nita: Wow, thank you so much, Bunga! It is a very beutiful necklace.

Bunga: No problem. I'm glad you like it!

Nita: It's so pretty, and it matches my birthday dress perfectly.

Bunga: I also got another present for you, but you have to open it tomorrow at the party.

Nita: Oh, what is it?

Bunga: It's a suprise. But I know you will like it.

Nita: Really? You're making me more excited for tomorrow.

Bunga: Mom and Dad have a lot more presents for you. I bet your friends do too.

Nita: Yes, I'm very excited. Thank you for the necklace and the present, Bunga. I'm very happy.

Bunga: No problem. Happy early birthday, sis!

Apologizing

*On the phone*

Laura: Hey, dad. I'm sorry but I can't come to the family event tomorrow.

Dad: Is that so? Why?

Laura: I have to study for a math test.

Dad: You didn't tell me you are going to have a math test, Ra.

Laura: I know, dad. I'm sorry. The teacher just announced it to our class today.

Dad: Alright, I will tell your aunt and uncle about it, I'm sure they will understand.

Laura: Thanks, dad. Tell them I'm sorry.

Dad: It's okay, Ra. Study hard for the test okay? I want you to get a good score.

Laura: Yes dad, I will study now.

Dad: And I will go back to work. Bye, Laura.

Laura: Bye, dad.
