amping
Last weekend, my friends and I went camping. We reached the camping ground after we walked for about one and a half hour from the parking lot. We built the camp next to a small river. It was getting darker and colder, so we built a fire camp.
 The next day, we spent our time observing plantation and insects while the girls were preparing meals. In the afternoon we went to the river and caught some fish for supper. At night, we held a fire camp night. We sang, danced, read poetry, played magic tricks, and even some of us performed a standing comedy.
On Monday, we packed our bags and got ready to go home.

12. buatlah karangan bahasa sunda dengan tema berkemah/camping


Ti mimiti SMP nepi ka SMA kuring sok ka ajak wae milu acara camping, boh dina acara camping Pramuka atawa PMR (Palang Merah Remaja), di tingkat Kota atawa Provinsi. Kulantaran acara camping eta mangrupa wakil ti sakola atawa kota, biasana sok diayakeun seleksi heula saha bae nu kapilih jadi wakil sakola atawa kota. Naon sababna kuring sok kaajak wae dina acara camping Pramuka jeung PMR ?.

Sanajan kuring kaasup anggota Pramuka jeung PMR oge, ari nu sabenerna mah kuring teh teu pati aktif dina kagiatan Pramuka jeung PMR teh, tapi pangna kuring sok kaajak acara camping Pramuka jeung PMR teh kulantaran kuring boga karesep ngagambar jeung maen musik utamana gitar. Pan ari dina salah sahiji acara camping Pramuka jeung PMR teh sok diayakeun lomba ngagambar atawa lomba musik. Tah lamun aya lomba ngagambar atawa musik, kuring kakara aktif dina dua lomba eta. 

Eta nu pikaseurieun mah, kulantaran kuring teu pati aktif dina kegiatan inti Pramuka jeung PMR, nya dina acara intina mah kuring sering kabagian peran pembantu umum, sanajan kitu oge kuring sok ngarasa resep mimilu acara camping Pramuka jeung PMR teh. Lamun dina acara Pramuka kuring sok kabagian peran pembantu umum, kitu oge dina acara PMR, seseringna mah kuring kabagian peran jadi pasen PMR, ari babaturan mah cape ngagogotong pasen, nya kuring mah ngeunah bae digogotong.

33. tulis sebuah surat untuk teman mu mengenai camping dalam bahasa inggris?​


Jawaban Dear: Thomas

It has been a long time since we exchanged letters. In this letter I am giving you an exhilarating experience of my camping trip.

I, along with six school mates, went camping to Bir in Himachal Pradesh. It was a seven-day trip. We went to Delhi by train and after a day's stay left for Bir early in the morning by bus and reached there by evening. We camped out in the woods. The nights were mostly clear and we enjoyed the beauty of the moon, the stars and the sky, It was wonderful to see the night sky, as there was not an iota of pollution in the hill area. We also sat around the camp fire and told stories and jokes.
