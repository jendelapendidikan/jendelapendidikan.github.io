21. perbedaan showing care, attention, symphaty beserta contohnya ?


1. Showing care adalah sebuah kalimat yang menyatakan simpati terhadap seseorang. Contoh: A: I'm sorry to hear that. B: I appreciate your thoughts and prayers. 2. Attention adalah sebuah kalimat yang menyatakan perhatian terhadap seseorang sedangkan Intention adalah kalimat perencanaan. 3. Shympaty sama halnya dengan showing care yaitu ekspresi kesedihan yang ditunjukan untuk teman yang sedang mendapat masalah contoh: our friend is sad because his/her grandfather is sick.1. Showing Care:Showing Care (Menenjukkan Keperdulian) adalah perasaan dimana seseorang peduli kepada orang yang lainnya.

A: I am not feeling well today.
B: I hope you get well soon!
A: Thank you.
B: You're welcome!

2. Attention:Attention (Perhatian) adalah perasaan dimana kita inging di amati oleh orang lain

A: Hey! Pay attention to me!
B: Oh, sorry!

3. Sympathy:Sympathy (Simpati/Belas Kasih) adalah perasaan dimana kita merasa sedih untuk orang lain.

A: Are you sad?
B: Yes, I am.
A: Why?
B: My mother died today.
A: Oh my God, I'm sosorry!
B: It's okay.

36. apa beda nya showing care & complimenting?? jelaskan detail beserta contoh


COMPLIMENTING (Giving Praise) Complimenting an expression of respect or praise of others. Complimenting very useful to give the spirit so that the person will retain preatasinya. There are several examples of the right time to reveal it, namely: In a general discussion When someone does something great When you visit a friend's house for the first time When you notice something new on one's appearance SHOWING CARE / Sympathy (Shows Concern) Sympathy is an expression or a feeling of pity and annoyance when we see people who are not lucky, have a problem, or in poor condition. Her aim is to make people feel comfortable that the problem with you. There are many ways that we can express it, namely: Mengiriminya letter SMS Mengiriminya Mengiriminya E-mail Atau directly meet
