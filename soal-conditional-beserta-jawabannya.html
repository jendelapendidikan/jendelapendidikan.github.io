5. jelaskan apa itu zero &first conditionaldan bagaimana cara mengerjakan soal zero&first conditional​


Jawaban:

Kalimat zero conditional

Digunakan untuk fakta ilmiah dan kebenaran/peraturan umum. Struktur: “If + present tense” untuk klausa kondisional, dan present tense untuk klausa utama.

Kalimat first conditional

Digunakan untuk membahas hal-hal yang mungkin terjadi di masa depan. Digunakan untuk menggambarkan hal-hal yang mudah terjadi atau diwujudkan. Struktur: “if + present tense” untuk klausa kondisional, dan future tense “will + infinitive” untuk klausa utama.

1. If you study with all of your effort, you will definitely pass the exam.

- The correct verb form is "study" as it matches the subject "you" and the sentence is in the present tense.

2. If I could speak English better, I would go to Florida.

- The correct verb form is "go" as it matches the subject "I" and the sentence is in the present conditional tense.

3. If my father bought me a car, I would be happy.

- The correct verb form is "bought" as it matches the subject "my father" and the sentence is in the past conditional tense.

4. If I had understood her, she would have left me alone.

- The correct verb form is "left" as it matches the subject "she" and the sentence is in the past conditional tense.

5. If I had met her, I would have taken her to my house and met my parents.

- The correct verb form is "met" as it matches the subject "I" and the sentence is in the past conditional tense.

6. If I were you, I would attend that International conference.

- The correct verb form is "attend" as it matches the subject "I" and the sentence is in the present conditional tense. The verb "were" is used instead of "am" because it is a hypothetical situation.

7. If I had read the mathematics materials, I would understand this formula well.

- The correct verb form is "understand" as it matches the subject "I" and the sentence is in the past conditional tense.

8. If the police had come earlier, they would have arrested the burglar.

- The correct verb form is "come" as it matches the subject "the police" and the sentence is in the past conditional tense.

9. I would have sat, if you had stood up.

- The correct verb form is "stood" as it matches the subject "you" and the sentence is in the past conditional tense.

- The correct verb form is "understood" as it matches the subject "I" and the sentence is in the past conditional tense.

14. 50 soal serta jawaban tentang conditional sentence ?


Conditional sentence is a sentence that expresses a condition. A condition is something that can only happen IF something else occurs. Conditional sentences are made up of a dependent clause and an independent clause joined to express said condition.

Pembahasan

Conditional Sentence Type 1: It is possible and also very likely that the condition will be fulfilled.

Conditional Sentence Type 2: It is possible but very unlikely, that the condition will be fulfilled.

Conditional Sentence Type 3: It is impossible that the condition will be fulfilled because it refers to the past.

Contoh soal tentang conditional sentences yaitu

Conditional sentence type 1:

1. If we ... (not/work) harder, we ... (not pass) the exam.

2. If she ... (not go) to the meeting, I ....(not go) either.

3. If the teacher ... (give) us lots of homework this weekend, I ... (not be) happy.

4. If the children ... (not eat) soon, they ... (be) grumpy.

5. If I ... (not go) to bed soon, I ... (be) tired in the morning.

6. If John .... (drink) too much coffee, he ... (get) ill.

7. If she ...(go) to the library, she ... (study) more.

8. If they ... (not/arrive) soon, we ... (be) late.

9. If you ... (buy) the present, I ... (wrap) it up.

10. If Lucy ... (not quit) her job soon, she ... (go) crazy.

Conditional sentence type 2:

1. If she ... (have) her laptop with her, she ... (email) me.

2. If Lucy ... (have) enough time, she ... (travel) more.

3. If I ... (want) a new car, I ... (buy) one.

4. If Jose ... (not speak) good French, he ...(not move) to Paris.

5. If the children ... (be) in bed, I ... (be able to) have a bath.

6. If you ... (not be) so stubborn, we ... (not have) so many arguments!

7. If you ... (arrive) early, it ... (be) less stressful.

8. If Julie ... (like) chocolate, I ... (give) her some.

9. If Luke ... (live) in the UK, I ... (see) him more often.

10. If we ... (not be) so tired, we ... (go) out.

Conditional sentence type 3:

1. If the students ... (not be) late for the exam, they ... (pass).

2. If the weather ... (not be) so cold, we ... (go) to the beach.

3. If the baby ... (sleep) better last night, I ...(not be) so tired.

4. If we ... (tidy) our flat, we .. (not lose) our keys.

5. If Luke ... (not send) flowers to his mother, she ... (not be) happy.

6. If Julie ... (not go) to Sweden, she ... (go) to Germany.

7. If we ... (not have) an argument, we ... (not be) late.

8. If I ... (not go) to the party, I ... (not meet) Amanda.

9. If the children ... (not eat) all that chocolate, they ... (feel) sick.

10. If she ... (study) Mandarin, she ... (go) to Beijing.

26. conditional sentense beserta contoh


CONDITIONAL SENTENCES

Conditional Sentences’ adalah kalimat pengandaian, yaitu:

A. REAL CONDITIONAL SENTENCES/CONDITIONAL TYPE ONE

FORM: (IF + THE SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE + THE FUTURE TENSE)
Real conditional sentences adalah kalimat yang akan terjadi atau tidak akan terjadi (mungkin terjadi) tergantung pada suatu kondisi atau keadaan
CONTOH:
Conditional sentences which may or may not happen (kalimat ‘conditional’ yang mungkin terjadi dan mungkin juga tidak terjadi)
• If it rains, I will not go
(We do not know yet wheter it will rain, may be it will rain. Maybe it won’t)
• If you insist, he will let you have it
(we do not know yet wheter you will insist. Maybe you will insist. Maybe you won’t)
• If she loves you, she will be happy for you.
(we do not know yet wheter she will love you. Maybe she will love you. Maybe she won’t).
“Kalimat utama di atas akan terjadi jika kondisi dalam If-clause terpenuhi. Kalau kondisi pada If-clause tidak terpenuhi, maka main clause-nya tidak akan terjadi.

B. UNREAL CONDITIONAL SENTENCES IN THE PRESENT/CONDITIONAL TYPE TWO

FORM: (IF + THE SIMPLE PAST TENSE + THE PAST FUTURE TENSE)
‘Unreal Conditional Sentences in the present’ adalah kalimat yang tidak sesuai dengan kenyataan yang ada sekarang di hadapan kita.
CONTOH:
Conditional sentences which is contrary to the fact at present (kalimat ‘conditional’ yang bertolak belakang dengan kenyataan saat ini)
• If I were in town, I would go.
(I am not in town)
• If I took the plane, I would get there fast.
(I don’t take the plane)
• If I knew, I would tell you.
(I don’t know)

C. UNREAL CONDITIONAL SENTENCES IN THE PAST

FORM: (IF + THE PAST PERFECT TENSE, + THE PAST FUTURE PERFECT TENSE)
‘Unreal conditional sentence in the past’ adalah kalimat yang tidak sesuai dengan kenyataan yang terjadi di masa lampau.
CONTOH:
• If I had know it, I would have told you.
(I didn’t know it, or I haven’t known it.)
• If you had told me, I would have defended you.
(You didn’t tell me, or you haven’t told me.)
• If she hadn’t come late, She would have defended you.
(She came late, or she has come late.)
