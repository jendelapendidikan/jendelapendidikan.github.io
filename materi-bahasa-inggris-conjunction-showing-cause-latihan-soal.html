- Introduction:

Conjunctions merupakan salah satu kelas kata yang menghubungkan kalimat, frasa, klausa atau dua kata menjadi satu kalimat atau tidak terpisah yang memiliki hubungan dekat antara kedua-duanya. Menggunakan kata hubung/conjunction agar teks yang kita tulis bisa berkembang dengan baik dan tidak terjadi pemborosan kata.

Jenis-jenis conjunctions dan contohnya:

Coordinating conjunctions: for, and, nor, but, or, yet, dan soon. Supaya piece-a-cake, ingat fanboys ya..  :DSubordinating conjunctions: although, before, as, etc.Correlative conjunctions: both... and, not only... but also, either... or, neither... nor, etc.

Parallelism merupakan komponen di dalam sebuah kalimat yang memiliki makna dan pengucapan yang sama. Parallelism terjadi repetisi (pengulangan) dengan makna sama seperti like father, like son. Kita dapat menggunakan konjungsi dalam kaliamat yang paralel dan untuk menghindari pengulangan menjadi like father and son. Perlu diingat juga, parallelism tidak dapat berkembang seperti like father and like son (pemborosan kata like) apabila kalimatnya ada konjungsinya (cth: and).

10. Dialog bahasa inggris untuk 4 orang materi asking for repetition, showing attention,showing admiration


Explanation:

Asking for repetition is an expression which is used when we hear something unclearly and want our partner to repeat what they said.
Expression of asking for repetition:
- What did you say?
- Pardon?
- Sorry, can you repeat it?
- I'm sorry, I didn't hear you.
- Sorry, I don't follow you.

Showing attention is an expression which is used to show that you are interested in the topic that your speaking partner is talking about.
Expression of showing attention:
- Really?
- Are you serious?
- Tell me more about it.
- And then what?
- What's next?

Showing admiration is an expression which is used to show that you admire someone's work, achievement, or appearance.
Expression of showing admiration:
- What a...!
- How...!
- It is awesome!
- That is amazing!
- Wow. You look ...!

14. Sebutkan 3 jenis conjunction (kata penghubung) yang ada dalam materi cause and effect yang telah kalian pelajari! *


Jawaban:

3 jenis conjuction (kata penghubung) dalam cause and effect (sebab dan akibat) yang paling sering digunakan adalah because, as, since dan so. Kata penghubung "because" dan since" digunakan untuk mengungkapkan "sebab" dan kata penghubung "so" digunakan untuk mengungkapkan "akibat"

Penjelasan:

Kata penghubung atau conjunction adalah kata-kata yang digunakan untuk menghubungkan kata lain, frasa dan kalimat. Salah satu fungsi conjuction adalah untuk menyatakan sebab akibat.

Contoh:

"I was sick" --> Sebab

"I have to rest" --> Akibat

Kalimat menggunakan conjuction menjadi:

"I have to rest because I was sick"

"Since I am sick, I have to rest"

"I have to rest as I am sick"

"I am sick, so I have to rest"

17. contoh soal pilihan ganda bahasa inggris cause and effect


Cause and Effect

Anna: Hey Lulu, I recognize that your face is looking brighter and your look slimmer! What’s the secret?

Lulu: Well, thanks for noticing Ann. For the past months I have been working out regularly at least three times a week. Thanks to yoga!

Anna: Wow, sounds very interesting.

Lulu: Yes, but not only it. I also have been taking care of my food. Due to my lactose intolerant, I try to avoid dairy as much as possible. And it works for me!

Anna: I’m very inspired Ann! As a result of my laziness and eating too much junk food, I gained 5 kilograms in a month! I need to get back in shape and do more workout.

Lulu: Yes, I support you!

Pembahasan

Untuk mengungkapkan cause and effect dalam bahasa Inggris, kita menggunakan signal words berikut:

due tobecause ofowing tothanks toas a consequence ofas a result ofbecausesinceasfor

Nah, untuk menunjukan cause and effect dalam kalimat ada dua jenis yakni yang diikuti kata benda (followed by noun) dan yang diikuti kata kerja (followed by verb).

Followed by noun

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Due to Jen’s laziness, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: Due toCause: Jen’s lazinessEffect: She didn’t pass the test

He was sick as a consequence of his ignorance.

Signal phrase: As a consequenceCause: His ignoranceEffect: He was sick

Followed by verb

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Because Jen was lazy, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: BecauseCause: Jen was lazyEffect: She didn’t pass the Math test

As he ignored his health, he will be sick,

Signal word: AsCause: He ignoredEffect: He will be sick

CONJUNCTION  

Conjunction adalah kata hubung yang digunakan untuk menggabungkan suatu kata, phrase, klausa atau kalimat menjadi sebuah kalimat. Dalam menggunakan kata hubung dalam sebuah kalimat yang menyatakan clause and effect dapat menggunakan beberapa kata berikut ini:  

1. So (Terlalu)  

Contoh:  

She ate too much this morning, so she fell asleep in the afternoon. (Ia terlalu banyak makan pagi ini, jadi ia tertidur siangnya.)  

2. As (Karena)  

Contoh:  

She deserved to get it, as she worked hard for it . (Ia layak mendapatkannya, karena dia telah bekarja keras untuk itu.)  

3. Since (Karena)  

Contoh: Since his father told him to study hard for his future so, he studies abroad now. (Semenjak ayahnya berkata kepadanya untuk belajar dengan giat , Ia bersekolah di luar negeri sekarang.)  

4. Because (Karena)  

Contoh:  

They go to hospital because my uncle gets in an accident. (Mereka pergi kerumah sakit karena pamanku kecelakaan.)  

5. Due to (Karena)  

Contoh:  

The young man was lost his foot, due to car accident. (Lelaki muda itu kehilangan kakinya, karena kecelakaan mobil)  

6. Hence (Karenanya, karena itulah, oleh karenanya)  

Contoh:  

He wants to take a leak; hence, he looks so hurry. (Ia ingin buang air kecil; karena itulah, ia terlihat amat terburu-buru.)  

7. Consequently (sebagai akibatnya/hasilnya)  

Contoh:  

He was a street racer back in that day; consequently, his broken legs. (Ia seorang pembalap jalanan kala itu; konsekuensinya, kakinya yang patah.)  

8. Therefore (Oleh karenanya)  

Contoh:  

1. She spent hours for just writing that article. Therefore, it could be finished before the deadline. (Ia menghabiskan waktu berjam-jam untuk menulis artikel itu. Karena nya, itu bisa diselesaikan sebelum tenggatnya.)  

2. They never argued about my decisions. Therefore, we always ended up as a great team. (Mereka tidak pernah mengeluh tentang keputusan-keputusanku. Karena itulah, kita selalu berakhir sebagai team yang baik.)  

9. As a result (Hasilnya, sehingga)  

Contoh:

He always saves all his earnings. As a result, his big luxury uphill mansion. (Ia selalu menabung semua pemasukannya. Sehingga, Puri besar dan megah diatas tebing miliknya.)  

10. For this reason (Karena alasan itulah)

Contoh:

1. He can play that game in days without doing anything else. For this reason, I call him ‘maniac’. (Ia mampu memainkan permainan ini berhari-hari tanpa melakukan apapun. Karena alasan inilah, aku menyebut dia ‘maniak’.)

2. My aunt always hugs me so hard. For this reason, I always hide when she comes to my place. (Bibiku selalu memelukku dengan erat. Karena alasan inilah, aku selalu bersembunyi ketika ia datang ketempatku.)
