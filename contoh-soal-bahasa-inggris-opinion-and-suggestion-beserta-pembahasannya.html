Suggestions dan soal serta pembahasannya

Materi Suggestion

Penggunaan Suggestions

Suggestions dapat digunakan dalam bentuk solutions, saran, rencana dan idea. Misalnya teman anda sedang dalam masalah tidak bisa mata pelajaran bahasa Inggris maka anda dapat menyarankannya untuk mengikuti kursus bahasa inggris

Expressions of giving suggestions

Expresi dibawah ini sering digunakan untuk membuat saran 
Let's ……… 
Why don't we ……… 
We could ……… 
What about ……… 
How about ……… 
I suggest that ……… 
You might want to change ……… 
I think ……… 
I don't think ………

Example of using Suggestions in sentences


- Let's go to the library. 
- Let's go to movies. 
- Why don't you do your homework before going out? 
- We could eat at home today. 
- What about eating at the new place? 
- How about going to Sam's place first? 
- I suggest that we call it a day. 
- You need to change your sleeping habits.
- I think you should go and meet her. 
- I think we should do it this way. 
Would you like to … ? 
Would you mind going to the city centre ? 
What do you think about going to the swimming pool ? 
Let’s go to the swimming pool!

Responding Suggestions


Yes, it’s a great idea. 
I agree. 
It sounds great. 
Yes, let’s do that/it. 
Super! OK! All right!

Responding and declining suggestions examples


1. Let’s watch football at the stadium 
Accepting : yes, Let’s go 
Declining : No, thank you I have other schedule, I must clean the house now 
2. Why you don’t have breakfast before go to school ? 
Accepting : that is a good idea 
Declining : I think I will be late, so I will have a breakfast at school 
3. How about go to the movie ? 
Accepting : O. K it is sound great 
Declining : No, I do not have money to buy the ticket 
4. I think you should do your homework now 

Accepting : yes, you are right 
Declining; take it easy my friend, I still have so much time

9. Berikan 3 contoh soal esay dan jawaban tentang suggestion dan opinion?


Expression Giving Suggestion and Opinion

Expression Giving Suggestion

Expression giving suggestion adalah ekspresi untuk memberikan saran kepada orang lain, biasanya menggunakan should/had better/ought to/how about atau why don't. Untuk lebih jelasnya berikut contoh dalam kalimat :

You should study hard for the coming exam.Why don't you go home early tonight?You had better tell him the truth.

Asking and Giving Opinion Expression

Dalam asking opinion atau menanyakan tentang pendapat seseorang mengenai suatu hal, phrase yang sering digunakan sebagai berikut :

What is your opinion about....What do you think/reckon?Do you have any idea..Do you have any commentsDo you agree with me?What are your thoughts on that?Would you agree with me that...

Giving Opinion

Dalam giving opinion atau memberikan pendapat, phrase yang sering digunakan diantaranya :

In my opinion/in my humble opinion..I reckon/I think..From my point of viewAs far as I concernedIn my viewI believe/I feel/certainI am surePembahasan

20. 10 contoh soal dan jawaban tentang giving suggestion (dalam bahasa inggris)


Giving suggestion (memberikan saran) diucapkan oleh seseorang kepada lawan bicaranya dengan tujuan untuk menyampaikan saran atau masukan kepada lawan bicaranya, agar orang tersebut bersedia melaksanakan saran atau masukannya. Saran atau suggestion yang diberikan ini bisa berupa nasihat, pendapat, gagasan, dan yang semacamnya.

Beberapa contoh ekspresi yang sering digunakan untuk giving suggestion di antaranya adalah:

I think .......

What about .............

What if ....................

You should ....................

Let's ..............................

Maybe you want to ............................

How if .......

How about .....

34. Contoh soal tentang expression suggestion dalam bahasa inggris


Giving Suggestion

Giving suggestion atau memberi saran adalah ungkapan yang digunakan seseorang untuk memberikan saran atau nasihat kepada orang lain.

Pattern yang digunakan untuk giving suggestion adalah:

Would you be so kind enough to + verb

Contoh kalimat:

Would you be so kind enough to play with me?

Would you be so kind enough to watch movie with me?

I would be so grateful if you would.....

Contoh kalimat:

I would be so grateful if you would play me.

I would be so grateful if you would watch movie with me.

How/What about... + Gerund

Contoh Kalimat:

How about watching movies?

How about playing football?

Suppose

Contoh kalimat:

Suppose that you want to play with me.

Suppose that you want to watch movies me.
