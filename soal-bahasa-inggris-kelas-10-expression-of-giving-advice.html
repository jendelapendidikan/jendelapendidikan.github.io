1. 10 expression of giving some advice and responses


Expression of giving advice and responses merupakan ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan nasihat atau saran kepada orang lain serta merespon nasihat atau saran tesebut. Berikut penjelasannya.

Penjelasan:

Expressing advice pada dasarnya sama dengan expressing suggestion. Advice dan suggestion sama-sama mengacu pada hal yang sebaiknya dilakukan oleh orang lain. Sedangkan respoding advice and suggestion mengacu pada respon orang lain terhadap nasihat atau saran yang diberikan, respon tersebut dapat berupa penerimaan maupun penolakan.

Expressions of Giving Advice

1. You should … (contoh: you should sleep earlier)

2. You ought to … (contoh: you ought to have breakfast)

3. I advise you to … (contoh: I advise you to study hard)

4. If I were you, I would … (contoh: if I were you, I would ask my friend how to solve this problem)

5. My advise is to … (contoh: my advice is to see a doctor)

6. You had better … (contoh: you had better tell her the truth)

7. I suggest that you … (contoh: I suggest that you drink more water)

8. Why don’t you … ? (contoh: why don’t you go to the dentist?)

9. How about … ? (contoh: how about cooking fried rice for dinner?)

10. Have you thought about … ? (contoh: have you thought about giving her a jacket?)

Expressions of Responding Advice

1. I think so. (accepting)

2. That’s a good idea. Thanks. (accepting)

3. Thank you. (accepting)

4. I’ll do that. (accepting)

5. Why didn’t I think of that? (accepting)

6. I don’t think so (refusing)

7. That’s not a good idea (refusing)

8. I doubt about it (refusing)

9. I’m not sure about your idea (refusing)

10. Thanks, but I don’t think it will help because … (refusing)

6. 10 contoh soal dan jawaban tentang giving suggestion (dalam bahasa inggris)


Giving suggestion (memberikan saran) diucapkan oleh seseorang kepada lawan bicaranya dengan tujuan untuk menyampaikan saran atau masukan kepada lawan bicaranya, agar orang tersebut bersedia melaksanakan saran atau masukannya. Saran atau suggestion yang diberikan ini bisa berupa nasihat, pendapat, gagasan, dan yang semacamnya.

Beberapa contoh ekspresi yang sering digunakan untuk giving suggestion di antaranya adalah:

I think .......

What about .............

What if ....................

You should ....................

Let's ..............................

Maybe you want to ............................

How if .......

How about .....

Contoh 10 pertanyaan pilihan ganda tentang giving suggestion.

1. Tom looks very tired. What's your suggestion to him?

a. you should eat more.

b. you should study harder

c. I think you ought to get some rest

d. what if you go for a walk?

jawaban: c

2. Linda is working on her homework, but she finds some difficult questions. What will you suggest her?

a. I think you should stop studying.

b. maybe you can sleep now.

c. What if you drink to refresh yourself?

d. what about going to your friend's house to study together?

jawaban: d

a. you must

b. you have

c. I think you should

d. what if we

a. you should take a rest.

b. what if you study harder?

c. what about calling your friend for help?

d. you had better call the police.

a. I think we should go to the town square for sight seeing.
