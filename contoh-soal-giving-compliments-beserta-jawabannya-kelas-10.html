10. tulisan 10 contoh soal pilihan ganda giving instruction beserta jawabannya ​


10 contoh soal Pilihan ganda dan jawabannya:

1. In which sentence is the instruction clear and easy to follow?

a. Please do the thing.

b. Turn the knob to the right until you hear a click.

c. The task should be completed.

d. Make sure to follow the procedure.

Jawaban: b. Turn the knob to the right until you hear a click.

2. What is the first step in assembling the furniture?

a. Read the instructions thoroughly.

b. Start with the biggest piece.

c. Gather all the tools.

d. Call for help.

Jawaban: a. Read the instructions thoroughly.

3. Which word is commonly used to begin an instruction?

a. Why

b. Therefore

c. First

d. Afterward

Jawaban: c. First

4. What should you do if you can't understand the instructions?

a. Ignore them.

b. Ask for clarification or help.

c. Give up.

d. Make your own instructions.

Jawaban: b. Ask for clarification or help.

5. Which of the following is NOT an example of a clear instruction?

a. "Add two cups of flour and mix well."

b. "Umm...do something with the ingredients."

c. "Stir the mixture gently for 3 minutes."

d. "Place the item in a preheated oven at 350°F."

Jawaban: b. "Umm...do something with the ingredients."

6. What is the best way to ensure someone follows your instructions?

a. Shout at them.

b. Write them in a language they don't understand.

c. Use clear and concise language.

d. Provide too many details.

Jawaban: c. Use clear and concise language.

7. What's the most common way to indicate a step in a list of instructions?

a. Using the word "again."

b. Numbering the steps.

c. Repeating the same instructions.

d. Using complex vocabulary.

Jawaban: b. Numbering the steps.

8. When providing instructions, it's important to:

a. Be vague and unclear.

b. Assume everyone knows what to do.

c. Use simple language and be specific.

d. Never provide any examples.

Jawaban: c. Use simple language and be specific.

9. What is the purpose of giving clear instructions?

a. To confuse people.

b. To make the task more difficult.

c. To ensure the task is completed successfully.

d. To waste time.

Jawaban: c. To ensure the task is completed successfully.

10. In an emergency situation, what's the most important thing to do when giving instructions?

a. Speak slowly and calmly.

b. Use complicated language to sound important.

c. Yell loudly.

d. Provide clear and concise directions.

Jawaban: a. Speak slowly and calmly.

19. 10 contoh soal dan jawaban tentang giving suggestion (dalam bahasa inggris)


Giving suggestion (memberikan saran) diucapkan oleh seseorang kepada lawan bicaranya dengan tujuan untuk menyampaikan saran atau masukan kepada lawan bicaranya, agar orang tersebut bersedia melaksanakan saran atau masukannya. Saran atau suggestion yang diberikan ini bisa berupa nasihat, pendapat, gagasan, dan yang semacamnya.

Beberapa contoh ekspresi yang sering digunakan untuk giving suggestion di antaranya adalah:

I think .......

What about .............

What if ....................

You should ....................

Let's ..............................

Maybe you want to ............................

How if .......

How about .....

Contoh 10 pertanyaan pilihan ganda tentang giving suggestion.

1. Tom looks very tired. What's your suggestion to him?

a. you should eat more.

b. you should study harder

c. I think you ought to get some rest

d. what if you go for a walk?

jawaban: c

2. Linda is working on her homework, but she finds some difficult questions. What will you suggest her?

a. I think you should stop studying.

b. maybe you can sleep now.

c. What if you drink to refresh yourself?

d. what about going to your friend's house to study together?

jawaban: d

3. A: .............................. go to the cinema and watch a good film?

B: That's a good idea

a. you must

b. you have

c. I think you should

d. what if we

jawaban: d

4. A: I feel drowsy.

B: ........................................

a. you should take a rest.

b. what if you study harder?

c. what about calling your friend for help?

d. you had better call the police.

jawaban: a

5. This is Saturday night. What will you suggest your friend?

a. I think we should go to the town square for sight seeing.

b. what about going to the doctor?

c. you should speak to your teacher.

d. what if we take this to be repaired?

jawaban: a

6. Doctor: ...................... smoking if you don't want to get sick.

Patient: Yes sir, I will try.

a. what if you stop

b. you should stop

c. how about

d. maybe you can stop

jawaban: b

7. A: I am so hungry.

B: .......................................

a. I think you should take a rest.

b. You ought to read it more carefully.

c. Calm down! You should be more relaxed.

d. What about going to a restaurant?

jawaban: d

8. Mother: I want to cook an omelette.

You: ..............................

a. Let's buy some eggs.

b. Let's buy some syrup.

c. I think you should buy a ticket first.

d. I think you should go to a barbershop.

jawaban: a

9. Your friend needs a book for his homework. But he doesn't have enough money to buy it. What will you suggest him?

a. Maybe you can borrow from our seniors?

b. I think you shouldn't do the homework.

c. What if we go to the zoo?

d. You ought to get some rest.

jawaban: a

10. You and your friends are on a trip to a beach in Banyuwangi. However, none of you knows about the route the that beach. What will be your suggestion?

a. I think we can go home now.

b. What about going to a restaurant?

c. Maybe you can open Google Maps application on your phone.

d. You should read the instruction more carefully.

jawaban: c

26. Make a short dialogue about giving compliments


║COMPLIMENT║

Answer

{ You visit your friend's house for the first time }

Sara     : Please, come in, Dew !

Dew    : So, Is this your house, Sara?

Sara    : Absolutely right, Dew!

Dew     : Wow, your house is spacious and beautiful. Your front park is fulfilled by the rose flowers and kinds of trees. It looks shady. That’s wonderful view.

Sara   : Thank you so much. Please, follow me, Dew! I would like to show my room. ( walking to Sara’s room )

Dew   : You room is very neat, Sara! There are so many art pictures on the wall. Your room is great and cozy.

Sara   : Thank you so much, Dew!

Explanation

COMPLIMENTCompliment is used to reveal praise/admiration on people's general appearance/new look when we meet him/her for the first time, something people did, and something people have in order to evoke someone' spirit to be better in the future. It looks like a seduction but it has the positive purpose.Compliment/Pujian merupakan sebuah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan pujian/kekaguman atas penampilan/suatu hal lain dari orang yang baru ditemuinya pertama kali, atas sesuatu yang telah orang lakukan dan atas sesuatu yang orang miliki ditujukan untuk membangun semangat seseorang untuk lebih baik lagi ke depannya. Ini seperti rayuan tetapi sebenarnya memiliki tujuan positif.

GIVING OPINION EXPRESSIONS

Giving opinion expression adalah ekspresi yang digunakan untuk memberikan opini. Phrase yang sering digunakan antara lain:

In my opinionI believe that...I am certain (that)I am strongly believeI am sureI think

Phrase yang sering digunakan dalam asking opinion. misalnya

What do you think about?What's your opinion on...How do you feel about...Do you have any views on...In your opinion,...Would you agree that...Pembahasan

31. definisi giving and responding to compliments？​


Pengertian

Giving compliment = Memberi pujian (memuji)Responding to compliment = Menanggapi pujian (merespons)

Contoh Ungkapan Giving Compliment (Memuji):

You look great/nice/beautifulThat's great/perfect/goodI like your hat/clothes/bag/jewelry/eyes/dress.What a nice carSuch a beautiful flower

Terjemahan

Kamu terlihat hebat/bagus/cantik Itu bagus/sempurna/bagusAku suka topi/pakaian/tas/perhiasan/mata/bajumuMobil yang bagus Bunga yang indah

Contoh Ungkapan Responding to Compliment (Merespons):

Thank youThanksReally?Do you think so?

Terjemahan

Terima kasihTerima kasihBenarkah?Kamu pikir demikian?

Catatan

Ungkapan memuji sebenarnya bebas selama itu merupakan sebuah pujian. Responsnya pun juga demikian. Di atas hanyalah beberapa contoh yang saya sediakan.
