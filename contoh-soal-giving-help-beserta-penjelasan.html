29. tulisan 10 contoh soal pilihan ganda giving instruction beserta jawabannya ​


10 contoh soal Pilihan ganda dan jawabannya:

1. In which sentence is the instruction clear and easy to follow?

a. Please do the thing.

b. Turn the knob to the right until you hear a click.

c. The task should be completed.

d. Make sure to follow the procedure.

Jawaban: b. Turn the knob to the right until you hear a click.

2. What is the first step in assembling the furniture?

a. Read the instructions thoroughly.

b. Start with the biggest piece.

c. Gather all the tools.

d. Call for help.

Jawaban: a. Read the instructions thoroughly.

3. Which word is commonly used to begin an instruction?

a. Why

b. Therefore

c. First

d. Afterward

Jawaban: c. First

4. What should you do if you can't understand the instructions?

a. Ignore them.

b. Ask for clarification or help.

c. Give up.

d. Make your own instructions.

Jawaban: b. Ask for clarification or help.
