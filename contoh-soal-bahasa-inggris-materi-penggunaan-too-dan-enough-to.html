the meaning of TOO is terlalu e.g he is too small to carry those heavy boxes
the meaning of TOO in elliptical structure is juga e.g i am smart and he is too

the meaning of ENOUGH is cukup e.g he is smart enough to answer those difficult answers.
the meaning of TOO is TERLALU
and the meaning of ENOUGH is CUKUP

Function of TOO is to a higher degree than is desirable, permissible, or possible; excessively.
Function of ENOUGH is to the required degree or extent (used after an adjective, adverb, or verb); adequately.....

Jawaban:

Contoh dialog 3 orang bahasa Inggris  yg menggunakan too and enough:

Situation: Outside the class Dhea, Anita and Sita are talking about their coming holiday.

Dhea: Anita, what is your planing for this coming holiday?

Anita: I am going to my grandmother's house.

Dhea: Where is it? Are you going with your family?

Anita: Yes, I'm going there with my parents. My grandmother lives in Banjarmasin. It's too far to travel alone. But, then my parents will be back to Jakarta, I'll be staying in there for the whole holiday, my Mum says I'm already big enough to take care my self. Do you think I'm capable enough to take care of my self?

Dhea: Yes, sure. Your Mom is right.

Anita: What about you Dhea and Sita, what are your planning for holiday?

Dhea: Maybe, my family and I will visiting our family during weekend. It's too boring to stay at home the whole time.

Sita: I'll spend my holiday with my cousins. We are going to a school camp. The camp is not too far from here, but I think we will have enough fun during the camp.

Dhea: That's sound interesting!

Sita: Indeed, I'm looking forward for it!

Anita: Well, I think It's getting too late, I should be home by now. OK girls, enjoy your holiday. See you after holiday!

Sita & Dhea: Yes you too, enjoy your holiday!

Pembahasan

Kata 'too and enough' dalam bahasa Inggris menyatakan jumlah tertentu, terlalu banyak atau terlalu sedikit.

TOO = lebih dari yang diperlukan

ENOUGH = sesuai jumlah yang diperlukan

NOT ENOUGH = kurang dari yang diperlukan/tidak mencukupi.

----------------------------------------------------------

TOO + ADJECTIVE + INFINITY CLAUSE

Alex is too young to drive. (Alex masih terlalu muda untuk menyetir mobil = belum cukup umur)

The test is too difficult for me to pass. ( Ujiannya terlalu sulit untuk saya bisa lulus)

The tea is too sweet, she put too much sugar in it. (Tehnya terlalu manis, terlalu banyak gula)

Too dalam kalimat diatas menerangkan kata adjective yang mengikutinya, jumlahnya tidak sesuai (below or above the desire amount)

ADJECTIVE + ENOUGH + INFINITY CLAUSE

Jill is old enough to drive. (Jill cukup umur untuk menyetir)

The test is easy enough for me to pass (Ujiannya cukup mudah untuk saya bisa lulus)

The tea is sweet enough (Tehnya cukup manis)

Kata enough dalam kalimat diatas menerangkan bahwa adjective dalam kalimat tersebut jumlahnya sesuai (within what is desirable)

17. penjelasan materi too and enough bahasa inggris


too artinya terlalu banyak
enough artinya itu cukup banyak

maaf ya kalau slh?berdasarkan apa yang saya tahu,
Too = terlalu
Enough = Cukup

Too diikuti oleh kata sifat (adjective) atau kata keterangan (adverb)
Contoh:
1. I wear TOO MUCH make up
2. She is TOO TALL to join the competition
3. The tea is TOO COLD
4. We walk TOO SLOW

Enough diikuti oleh kata benda (noun)
Contoh:
1. I get ENOUGH MILK
2. She brings ENOUGH MONEY
3. He eats ENOUGH FOOD

Apabila Enough berada di belakang, (......+enough) maka kata Enough mengikuti kata sifat (adjective) atau kata keterangan (adverb)
Contoh:
1. I run FAST ENOUGH
2. My grandfather is OLD ENOUGH to retire
3. We were YOUNG ENOUGH to get the free ice cream

 menyatakan pernyataan negatif ( hal yang tidak mungkin, sesuatu yang berlebihan dari yang dinginkan )

Adjective  + enough

22. tolong tuliskan sebuah cerita singkat bahasa inggris menggunakan kata too,enough,very,so.


Kata too, very, so , dan enough dalam bahasa Inggris digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang lebih. Misalnya so beautiful, too beautiful, very beautiful, dan beautiful enough. Namun dalam segi arti yang terdapat dalam setiap kata-kata ini memiliki arti dan makna yang berbeda satu sama lainnya.  

PembahasanVERY

Very digunakan untuk membicarakan suatu hal yang mengandung makna atau arti ‘sangat’.

RUmusnya : VERY + ADJECTIVE

Contoh:

1. she is very kind girl (dia adalah gadis yang sangat baik)

2. I see a very handsome man in the street (aku melihat seorang pria yag sagat tampan di jalan)

3. it is very difficult task (ini merupakan sebuah tes yang sangat sulit)

4. he is very favourite actor (dia merupakan aktor favorit)

5. she is very friendly (dia sangat ramah)

TOO

Too memiliki arti terlalu, biasanya digunakan untuk hal-hal yang bermakna negative.

Rumusnya: TOO + ADJECTIVE

Contoh :

1. This soup is too salt for me (sup ini terlalu asin untuk ku)

2. this bread is too sweet for me (roti ini sangat manis untuk ku)

3. It is too difficult for me (ini terlalu sulit untuk ku)

4. Please bring this bag for me, it is too heavy for me (tolong bawakan tas ini untuk ku, ini terlalu berat)

5. This juice is too sweet for us (jus ini terlalu manis untuk kami)  

ENOUGH

Enough memiliki arti cukup, enough diletakkan setelah adjective atau kata sifat dalam bahasa inggris.

Rumusnya:  ADJCTIVE + ENOUGH

Contoh :

1. That woman is beautiful enough for us (wanita itu cukup cantik untuk kita)

2. the task is difficult enough for me (tes itu cukup sulit untuk saya)

3. this coffee is sweet enough for you (kopi ini cukup manis untuk mu)

38. tolong bantu, soalnya di kumpul besok buat diambil nilai. tuliskan sebuah paragraf tentang pengalaman yang menggunakan too dan enough dalam bahasa inggris


Recount Text using Too and Enough

I want to tell you a story about my failed holiday. So, a month ago me and my sister planned a holiday to Bali. We had our flight in the early morning around 6 a.m., but the night before we slept too late and didn’t get enough time to prepare ourselves.

Long story short, we made it to the taxi but unfortunately in the high way there was an accident which caused traffic. It was too long. We were afraid that we couldn’t catch our flight, even though there was enough time.

Finally we arrived fifteen minutes before boarding. My sister said “we are too late”, but I was insisting. And yes, she was right. The plane has departed. We were disappointed, and went back to our home.


Pembahasan

Recount text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memiliki fungsi untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lalu. Tujuannya untuk menghibur dan memberikan informasi bagi para pembaca.

Ada beberapa macam recount text, pada teks soal merupakan contoh personal recount yang menceritakan ulang pengalaman pribadi penulis.


Nah, untuk menggunakan kata too dan enough pada kalimat berikut adalah tata caranya:


Too digunakan untuk menunjukan bahwa sesuatu ‘lebih’ dari apa yang dibutuhkan.


too + adjective

This coffee is too hot. I can’t drink it.

too + adverb

He drives too fast. It’s dangerous!

too much + (uncountable) noun

This food is very sweet, it has too much sugar.

too many + (countable) noun

The class has too many students, some of them don’t have desks.

Enough digunakan untuk menunjukan bahwa sesuatu sudah ‘cukup’ dari apa yang dibutuhkan.


adjective + enough

He is very tall. He is tall enough to reach the top shelf.

adverb + enough

I think I am fast enough to run with her.

enough + noun

He has enough money to buy a new toy.
