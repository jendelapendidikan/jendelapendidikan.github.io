17. soal uts bahasa inggris tentang uang




1.       Untuk pertama kalinya uang digunakan masyarakat sebagai … 

a.       Alat tukar barang

b.      Alat bayar utang

c.       Alat pembentuk kekayaan

d.      Alat satuan hitung

2.       Berikut ini adalah fungsi turunan uang kecuali …  .

a.       Alat satuan hitung

b.      Alat pembayaran yang sah

c.       Alat penimbun kekayaan

d.      Alat pemindah kekayaan

3.       Dengan uang 7000, kita dapat membeli beras I kg. Nilai uang tersebut dinamakan dengan nilai …  .

a.       Nominal intrinsic

b.      Intrinsik

c.       Internal

d.      Eksternal

4.       Proses penyelesaian pembayaran antar bank dengan memindahkan saldo kepada pihak yang berhak menerimasnya disebut …  .

a.       Bank garansi

b.      Letter of credit

c.       Giro

d.      Kliring

5.       Di antara lembaga keuangan bukan Bank di bawahy ini yang bergerak di bidang pembiayaan barang barang modal adalah …  .

a.       Pegadaian

b.      Asuransi

c.       Financing

d.      Leasing

6.       Perusahaan asuransi yang menyelenggarakan jaminan untuk perusahaan terhindar dari musibah kecelakaan, termasuk pada asuransi …  .

a.       Asuransi jiwa

b.      Asuransi kebakaran

c.       Asuransi kecelakaan

d.      Asuransi kerugian

7.       Surat perjanjian bahwa seseorang mengikuti asuransi pada perusahaan asuransi disebut …  .

a.       Premi

b.      Polis

c.       Akte

d.      Segel

8.       PT. Taspen merupakan perusaahn negara yang mengelola …  .

a.       Saham BUMN

b.      Dana Pensiun

c.       Kredit perbankan

d.      Keuangan negara

9.       Dengan uang senilai 5.000.000 kita dapat membeli berbagai macam valuta asing. Nilai uang tersebut dinamakan nilai …  .

a.       Internal

b.      Eksternal

c.       Nominal

d.      Intrinsik

10.   Kebijakan pemerintah untuk menurunkan nilai mata uang dalam negeri terhadap mata uang asing dinamakan …  .

a.       Apresiasi

b.      Eksternal

c.       Revaluasi

d.      Depresiasi

Text 1 Early one morning, Umar was walking to the bus stop to catch a bus to school. A loud, screeching noise suddenly pierced the air. Umar realized that there had been an accident. A small boy was lying unconscious on the road, and the car that had hit him had already run away. Umar was confused and frightened. “is the boy dead?” Umar asked himself. However, the boy was still breathing. Umar could see that the boy had bruises all over his face and arms. Umar did not know what to do. Few cars passed along this road at this time of the day. The nearest house which he could go to for help was his own, but it would take ten minutes to walk there. Umar finally decided to stay with the boy until somebody came by. Time passed very slowly – so it seemed to Umar. The boy was still unconscious, and Umar prayed for the boy to regain consciousness. Umar sprang to his feet at the sound of an approaching car. He waved frantically at the driver to stop. Umar asked the driver,”You will help this boy, won,t you, Sir?” The driver was kind. He carried the injuried boy to the car. Umar went with them to the hospital. Later, the driver took Umar to the police station to report the accident. Umar had a lot to tell to his friends that day at school. All his friends though Umar was a very brave boy.”it was an unforgettable incident, wasn,t it?” They asked him. “Yes, it was, “Umar replied.”it was a terrifying experience, thought!”
