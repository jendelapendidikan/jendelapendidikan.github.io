1. contoh soal bahasa inggris menentukan noun dan verb


NOUN
Noun adalah kata benda, penggunaan kata benda dapat sebagai subject kalimat,object kata kerja dan objek kata depan.
Noun dapat berupa ,contoh nya :
• The names of person (nama-nama orang)
 General Soeharto
 Queen Victoria
 Prof.Dr.Amien Rais,M.A

• The names of places ( countries , cities ,etc)
 Japan
 Korea
 America
 Indonesia
• The names of things ( nama-nama benda)
 Table
 Cupboarrd
 Paper
 School

VERB
Verb adalah kata yang menunjukkan kegiatan atau kata bantuyang membantu kata lain,sehingga kata itu dapat menjadi predikat.
Contoh :
1. I take an English course twice a week.
2. He will come on time next week
3. They are learning English with new students
4. He is a giving speech

15. sangkuriang dalam bahasa inggris dan contoh soalnya


Once upon a time, a beautiful lady named Dayang Sumbi gave birth to a boy which she named Sangkuriang. When Sangkuriang was old enough to hunt, he took his mother’s dog Tumang (which according to this legend is an incarnation of a god and also Sangkuriang’s father) and ordered Tumang to chase after a boar. When Tumang did not follow Sangkuriang’s order, Sangkuriang became very angry and he killed Tumang. He carved out Tumang’s heart and brought it back to his mother.
Dayang Sumbi took the heart, cooked it and ate it. When she discovered that the heart belonged to Tumang, her husband, she was overcome with wrath. She sent her son away, but not before she hit him with a spoon and left a deep scar on his head.
Sangkuriang travelled around the world. After a long while, he arrived back in his village again without recognizing it. He saw a beautiful lady and fell in love with her. Little did he know that she was his own mother. He asked for her hand in marriage and she agreed.
Dayang Sumbi later realized that Sangkuriang was her son, as she recognized the scar that she had inflicted. She tried to tell him and break off the wedding, but he didn’t believe her and insisted to go on with the wedding. Dayang Sumbi then set an impossible condition that Sangkuriang has to fulfill in order to marry her: he should build her a big boat and a lake by damming Citarum river, all within one night, and it shall be finished by dawn. Sangkuriang agreed to the condition.
He built a boat from a large tree, and with the help of spirits, he dammed the Citarum river with landslides. The water eventually rose and filled the plain, turning it into a lake. When dawn was near, he was almost ready. Dayang Sumbi realized this, so she prayed for divine intervention. As an answer to her prayers, the eastern horizon lit up. Deceived by the lights, cocks crowed and farmers rose for the new day, thinking that dawn has broken.
Sangkuriang was also deceived. He thought his endeavor has failed. Angrily, he kicked the boat so that it toppled over. This boat became Tangkuban Parahu mountain (tangkuban means upside down, and parahu means boat). The pile of leftover woods became Mt. Burangrang, and the rest of the big tree became Mt. Bukit Tunggul. The lake became lake Bandung (which literally means ‘dam’).

The development of information and technology in this modern era is very rapid, as is the development of mushrooming gadgets. Gadgets are now cheap and easy to get, lots of people communicate using social media such as paths, facebook, Twitter, Instagram, BBM, and others. In this modern era people who are actively using social media will be considered cool and slang, whereas if people don't use social media they will be considered outdated.

Social media is used to exchange information, provide comments, share pictures and videos. The question is, will the increasing use of social media users guarantee increased social awareness? Many people are too busy with their own gadgets until many people who have two or more accounts in one social media, without realizing someone who uses excessive social media will have a bad influence.

Keep away from the close and get closer, one of the bad effects of social media users. Social media is very helpful in communication, social media users can communicate anytime and anywhere as long as they have an internet connection. But social media users will never know when users will get e-mails, announcements or other messages, so users will constantly check their gadgets. This will affect the ignorance of people and the surrounding environment, and can forget our obligations such as assignments and other activities, so that social media can make someone become anti-social.

Social media has a strong addiction effect. One day without opening social media will certainly be difficult. I will share three easy ways that can reduce social media users too much. The first way is to use social media as needed and start communicating directly to people around you, because talking directly gets more meaning such as intonation and body language. The second way is to log out. This method is effective because by logging out, social media users will wait for loading, requiring memory passwords and user IDs. So this will cause a sense of laziness to log in to social media.

Now the providers are competing to provide large data packages and affordable prices. However, we have to outsmart buying a small data package, so as to minimize the use of social media, such as Path and Instagram, which consume a lot of data packages. If we include people who use social media too much, try these three ways. The key to success is willingness and discipline.


30. Tuliskan definisi, contoh, jenis, fungsi sosial, Struktur teks, beserta fitur tata bahasa dari teks naratif... Pakai bahasa Inggris.......​


Jawaban:

Teks Prosedur adalah teks tulisa atau lisan yang berisi instruksi kepada pembaca atau pendengar untuk membuat atau melakukan sesuatu. Teks ini didisain untuk menggambarkan bagaimana sesuatu dicapai melalui serangkaian tindakan-tindakan atau langkah-langkah.

Oleh karena itu, teks ini bisa berbentuk rangkaian instruksi, petunjuk atau sebuah manual.

a. Fungsi Sosial

untuk menginformasikan kepada para pembaca atau pendengar bagaimana cara membuat atau melakukan sesuatu.

b. Struktur Teks

1. Goal (tujuan):

memberikan informasi berdasarkan tujuan dan maksud teks.

2. Materials:

Daftar bahan-bahan yang diperlukan untuk mencapai tujuan.

3. Steps:

Serangkaian urutan instruksi atau kegiatan untuk mencapai tujuan melalui urutan langkah-langkah yang benar.

c. Unsur kebahasaan

Terdapat beberapa ciri kebahasaan dalam Procedure Text, diantaranya yaitu:

1. Menggunakan pola kalimat imperative (Perintah), misalnya, Cut, Don’t mix, dsb.

2. Menggunakan action verbs, misalnya turn, put, don’t, mix, dsb.

3. Menggunakan connectives (kata penghubung) untuk mengurutkan kegiatan, misalnya then, while, dsb.

4. Menggunakan adverbials (kata keterangan) untuk menyatakan rinci waktu, tempat, cara yang akurat, misalnya for five minutes, 2 hours, dsb.

5. Menggunakan simple present tense
