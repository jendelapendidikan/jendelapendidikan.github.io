11. tulislah 10 nama binatang dan masing masing tulis ciri khususnya bahasa Inggrisnya​


Jawaban:

1. frog

Jumping

Frogs are generally recognized as exceptional jumpers and, relative to their size, the best jumpers of all vertebrates

Walking and running

Frogs in the families Phrynomantis Bifasciatus walking on a level surface Frogs in the families Bufonidae, Rhinophrynidae, and Microhylidae have short back legs and tend to walk rather than jump

2. cat

Vision

Cats have excellent night vision and can see at only one-sixth the light level required for human vision

Smell

Cats have an acute sense of smell, due in part to their well-developed olfactory bulb and a large surface of olfactory mucosa

3. fly

Flight

Flies are capable of great maneuverability during the flight due to the presence of the halteres. These act as gyroscopic organs and rapidly oscillated in time with the wings; they act as a balance and guidance system by providing rapid feedback to the wing-steering muscles, and flies deprived of their halteres are unable to fly.

4.shark

Electroreception

The ampullae of Lorenzini are the electroreceptor organs. The number in the hundreds to thousands. Sharks use the ampullae of Lorenzini to detect the electromagnetic fields that all living things produce. This helps sharks (particularly the hammerhead shark) find prey.

5. flea

Jumping

Their legs are long, the hind pair well adapted for jumping; a flea can jump vertically up to 18 cm (7 in) and horizontally up to 33 cm (13 in), making the flea one of the best jumpers of all known animals (relative to body size),

6. stingray

Venom

The venom of the stingray has been relatively unstudied due to the mixture of venomous tissue secretions cells and mucous membrane cell products that occur upon secretion from the spinal blade.

7. mosquito

Mouthparts

Mosquito mouthparts are very specialized, particularly those of the females, which in most species are adapted to piercing skin and then sucking blood. Apart from bloodsucking, the females generally also drink assorted fluids rich in dissolved sugar, such as nectar and honeydew, to obtain the energy they need.

8. elephant

Attacks

Elephants can exhibit bouts of aggressive behavior and engage in destructive actions against humans. In Africa, groups of adolescent elephants damaged homes in villages after cullings in the 1970s and 1980s. Because of the timing, these attacks have been interpreted as vindictive

9. Snake

Molting

Molting, or ecdysis, serves a number of functions. Firstly, the old and worn skin is replaced; secondly, it helps get rid of parasites such as mites and ticks

10. Crocodile

Olfaction

Crocodilian sense of smell has very well developed, aiding them to detect prey or animal carcasses that are either on land or in water, from far away. It is possible that crocodiles use olfaction in the egg prior to hatching.

39. 5 nama binatang beserta penjelasan yang lengkap dalam bahasa Inggris


Penjelasan:

Fish are members of poikilothermic vertebrae (cold blooded) that live in water and breathe with gills. the most diverse group of vertebrates with more than 27,000 species worldwide.

Elephants are large mammals of the family Elephantidae and the order Proboscidea. Elephants are herbivorous animals that can be found in various habitats, such as savannas, forests, deserts, and swamps. They tend to be near water.

Cats are a type of carnivorous mammal from the Felidae family. Cat habitat is on land that usually mingles with humans as pets and some live wild. The term cat is usually directed at small cat animals that have been tamed and maintained by humans.

Birds are members of vertebrate groups that have feathers and wings. The oldest bird fossil found in Germany and known as Archeopteryx. The types of birds are so varied, ranging from tiny hummingbirds to ostriches, which are taller than humans.

Rabbits are mammals from the family Leporidae, which can be found in many parts of the earth. Rabbits breed by breeding methods called viviparous. Formerly, this animal is a wild animal that lives in Africa to mainland Europe.
