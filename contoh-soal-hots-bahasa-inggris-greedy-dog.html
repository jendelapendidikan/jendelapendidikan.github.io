1)  

What’s the benefit of consuming NatraBurst?

A) Increase appetite

B) Decrease energy

C) Promote fat loss

D) Promote healthy hair

Pembahasan:

Untuk menjawab soal ini, kamu harus melakukan screening terhadap teks yang disajikan. Sebelumnya, pahamilah bahwa yang ditanyakan adalah keuntungan dari mengonsumsi NatraBurst. Pada teks, dituliskan bahwa dampak positif NatraBurst adalah: (1) increase energy, (2) reduce appetite, (3) promote fat loss, (4) anti-aging, dan (5) promote healthy skin. Dengan demikian, jawaban yang tepat adalah C.

2)

The purpose of the text is …

A) to warn the reader the danger of the product.

B) to describe the composition of the product.

C) to persuade people to consume the product.

D) to tell the reader how to use the product.

Pembahasan:

Kenalilah terlebih dahulu jenis teks yang disajikan. Teks tersebut merupakan descriptive text yang mendeskripsikan sebuah produk bernama NatraBurst. Deskripsi yang tertera berfungsi untuk mengiklankan produk tersebut. Sebuah iklan bertujuan untuk mengajak orang-orang untuk membeli/ mengonsumsi suatu produk. Dengan demikian, jawaban yang tepat adalah C.

21. how to make hot dog dalam bahasa inggris


Ingredients

5 pounds fully pastured/grass-fed beef (either ground by your butcher or in cubes for you to grind at home)
1 tablespoon paprika
2 tablespoons mustard powder
1 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
Pinch of celery seed
½ teaspoon coriander
2 tablespoons salt
Just over 1 cup ice-cold water
Sheep casings (24-26 mm)
Directions:

Mixing:

If you’ve purchased beef cubes and/or fat from your butcher, place them on a baking pan and place in your freezer. You don’t want to freeze the meat; you just want a crunchy exterior surface.
Once the meat is sufficiently cold, nest a nonreactive bowl into another containing ice (keeping things cold here) and quickly grind. If things start to warm up, you can grind a few small pieces of ice with your meat to bring the temp back down.
Add your spices to the ground meat and mix until thoroughly combined. You’ll also want to add just a bit of ice water here to help with the bind. You’ll know it’s mixed well when it becomes tacky and starts to stick to the bowl. Place the meat back into the freezer and set up your food processor.
Working quickly, place your meat and about half the ice water in the bowl of your food processor and begin to mix. Continue adding the ice water to this process, which should take no more than 5-6 minutes. If you’re like me, you’re constantly worried about breaking your emulsion, so work in batches, returning what’s been emulsified to the freezer. When you’re done emulsifying, it’s time to test both for taste and to ensure that the emulsion has not been broken.
Spoon a tablespoon of the farce into a pan and cook. Adjust your spices if needed, and if it exudes any water, your emulsion is broken. Let’s not let that happen. At this point you can cover your farce and place it in your refrigerator overnight. This allows for fuller flavor development as the meat proteins react with the spices.

Stuffing:

You’re now ready to stuff! You’ve kept your stuffer and its parts in the fridge in order to keep it cool and your farce has either been in the fridge overnight or in the freezer while you set up your stuffer.
Sheep casings are what you’ll use here as they are thinner and help with that traditional hot dog snap. I like a slightly larger one at about 24-26 mm, since they’re easier to work with. And of course, just as we do with our non-emulsified sausages, you’ll soak and flush them to remove any excess salt.
Place a sheet pan, with a bit of water on it, underneath the nozzle of your stuffer and quickly begin to fill the casings. The watered sheet pan helps the filled casings move easily away from the tip of the nozzle.
Once your casings are filled, twist off into desired lengths and it’s back into the fridge while you get your cooking implements set up.

Cooking:

You can poach, roast or smoke. You’re looking for an internal temp of around 145°-150°F. That’ll happen in a 170°F smoker in about an hour, in a 200°F oven in about 30 minutes, or, how I like to do them, in a water bath of 160°F for about 30 minutes. Once the sausages are fully poached, you’ll want to toss them into an ice bath to stop the cooking process.
Since they are fully cooked, you’re only looking to finish them, and that can be done in a few ways. My two favorites are once again poached, in something like sauerkraut, or sliced lengthwise and cooked on a flat cooking surface.
For the first method, pour the sauerkraut and its liquid into an aluminum pan, and along with the hot dogs bring to a bubble over direct heat. This should take about five minutes. Slide them over to the cool side of your grill and in about ten minutes, you’ll have perfectly heated, unwrinkly hot dogs! Pop them directly on the grill for a minute or two for a bit of char.
The second method is kind of self-explanatory. This will take about five minutes.
As for buns, let’s keep things simple here with the old standby — Martin’s potato roll. Save the split tops for your lobster. I don’t love to grill buns as I don’t need any extra crunch. I like them steamed: Place a wire rack over an aluminum tray of water and heat that water over direct heat on your grill. You’ve got steamed buns!

24. Orientation,complication,resolution,re-orientation dalam cerita the greedy dog​


The "The Greedy Dog" story has a narrative structure consisting of orientation, complications, resolution, and oriented. Here is a brief explanation of each element in the story:

1. Onboarding: At the start of the story, we were introduced to the very greedy dog's character. The dog always wants to have more food and never settle on what it has.

2. Complications: One day, the dog found a huge piece of meat. In his excitement, he carried the meat to the river bank and saw the shadow of another dog in the water. The greedy dog thought the shadow was another dog who also had meat. Without thinking, he jumped into the water to steal the flesh from the shadow. However, when he jumped up, the real meat fell into the water and went missing.

3. Resolution: The dog realized he had lost his flesh for his ferocity. He felt sorry and learned from his mistakes. He promised not to become greedy and act wiser in the future.

4. Re-oriented: After that incident, the dog became more wise and no longer greedy. He learned to appreciate what he has and is no longer tempted by greed. The dog lived happier and satisfied with what it has.

In the story "The Greedy Dog", orientation introduces the character and background of the story, complications create conflict, resolution resolve conflict, and re-oriented describes the character changes and learning that occur.

28. Orientation,complication,resolution,re-orientation dalam cerita the greedy dog​


Jawaban:

1. Orientasi: Pada bagian orientasi, cerita memperkenalkan pembaca pada latar belakang dan karakter utama cerita. Dalam cerita "The Greedy Dog", orientasi akan menjelaskan tentang anjng yang sangat rakus dan sering mencuri makanan dari tetangga-tetangganya.

2. Komplikasi: Bagian komplikasi adalah saat terjadi peristiwa atau masalah yang mengubah keadaan atau menghadirkan konflik dalam cerita. Dalam cerita ini, komplikasi terjadi ketika anjng rakus mencuri sepotong daging dari pasar dan berlari pulang dengan penuh kegembiraan. Namun, ketika ia melintasi jembatan, ia melihat bayangan anjng lain di air dan mengira itu adalah anjng lain yang memiliki potongan daging yang lebih besar. Dalam keinginannya untuk mendapatkan potongan daging yang lebih besar, anjng itu melompat ke air dan kehilangan potongan daging yang telah ia curi.

3. Resolusi: Bagian resolusi adalah saat masalah dalam cerita dipecahkan atau diselesaikan. Dalam cerita ini, resolusi terjadi ketika anjng rakus menyadari bahwa bayangan anjng di air adalah refleksinya sendiri. Ia kehilangan potongan daging yang telah ia curi dan merasa menyesal atas keganasannya. Ia belajar pelajaran berharga tentang keserakahan dan kehilangan.

4. Re-orientasi: Bagian re-orientasi adalah saat cerita memberikan penutup atau pesan moral kepada pembaca. Dalam cerita "The Greedy Dog", re-orientasi akan menekankan pentingnya bersikap tidak rakus dan bersyukur dengan apa yang kita miliki. Anjng itu belajar untuk tidak mencuri dan menjadi lebih bijaksana setelah pengalaman yang ia alami.

38. bagaimana cara membuat hot dog dalam bahasa inggris


There are some methods to make hotdog. You can grill it, boil it, heating it in microwave or oven, or even fry it. For me a grilled hotdog is the most delicious. You should try some!

1
Fire up your grill. Grilling hot dogs results in a pleasant smoky taste, and many consider this to be the best way to prepare them. Any sort of grill will work, so go ahead and light your charcoal, gas, or wood-fired grill. While the grill is heating up, prepare the hot dog buns and condiments. Hot dogs are best enjoyed hot from the grill. Make sure that one side of the grill is hot and one side of the grill is a little cooler. You can do this by stacking up the charcoal a little higher on one side. If you have a gas grill, you should be able to control the heat using the knobs on your appliance to make hot dogs good.

2
Lay the hot dogs on the cooler side of the grill. Place them at an angle to create a diagonal sear-mark on the dog.

3
Cook the hot dogs for a minute on each side. Hot dogs come already cooked, so your goal here isn't to cook the dog so much as give it some good color and get it piping hot without burning it.Keep turning the dogs until all sides have developed a nice, even char.If the dogs are hot but have not developed color, move them over to the hot side of the grill. Grill them quickly, just to develop a bit of char, and then move them to a plate.

4
Serve the hot dogs. Place them in a bun and serve with any combination of mustard, ketchup, relish, onion, tomato, cheese, or sauerkraut.How to Make Hot Dog

Ingredients and recipes used to how to make hot dogs

     5 pieces of hot dog buns
     5 pieces of beef sausage
     5 pieces of curly lettuce
     1 piece of cucumber, cut into 0.5 cm round
     3 pieces tomatoes, cut into 0.5 cm round
     1 piece of onion, cut into rings of 0.5 c
     margarine to taste

Ingredients and recipes for the sauce - how to make hot dogs

     mayonnaise 200 gr
     4 tablespoons tomato sauce
     onion chopped 1 tablespoon
     2 pieces of pickled cucumber, chopped

How to Make Hot Dog:

1. For the sauce: Mix all ingredients and stir well. set aside.
2. The sides of the bread lengthwise but do not break.
3. Heat the margarine in a non-stick omelet pan, bake bread and sausages until cooked and browned and lift.
4. open parts of the bread and spread with sauce to taste.
5. set up inside lettuce, tomato, cucumber, beef sausage and onions.
6. katupkan bread top, finished way of making hot dogs and serve warm with chili sauce if desired.
