31. contoh monolog bahasa Inggris yang berhubungan dengan time​


Monolog: The Importance of Time Management

Time is a valuable resource that we all have in common. It is something that we cannot control, but we can certainly manage it. Time management is crucial in our daily lives, whether it's in school, work, or personal endeavors. In this monologue, I will share with you the importance of time management and how it can positively impact our lives.

Time is limited, and we must make the most of it. Imagine a scenario where you have an important project to complete. You have a deadline looming, and yet you find yourself procrastinating, wasting precious time on trivial activities. This lack of time management can lead to stress, missed opportunities, and compromised quality of work.

On the other hand, effective time management allows us to be more productive and achieve our goals. By setting priorities and creating a schedule, we can allocate time for important tasks, ensuring that they are completed in a timely manner. This not only reduces stress but also provides a sense of accomplishment and boosts our confidence.

Time management also helps us maintain a healthy work-life balance. By organizing our time efficiently, we can dedicate quality time to our family, friends, and personal hobbies. We can pursue our passions, engage in self-care activities, and foster meaningful relationships. It is important to remember that time spent with loved ones and doing things we enjoy is equally valuable as time spent on work or responsibilities.

Furthermore, time management allows us to seize opportunities. When we manage our time well, we create space for new experiences, personal growth, and learning. We can explore new interests, attend workshops or courses, and expand our knowledge and skills. Time management empowers us to make the most of every moment and make progress towards our aspirations.

In conclusion, time management is essential for a fulfilling and successful life. By managing our time effectively, we can reduce stress, increase productivity, maintain a healthy work-life balance, and seize opportunities. Let us embrace the value of time and make a commitment to manage it wisely. Remember, time is a precious gift, and it is up to us to make the most of it.

32. soal bahasa inggris tentang recount text kls 8


1. Recount Text
On Wednesday, my students and I went to Yogyakarta. We stayed at Dirgahayu Hotel which is not far from Malioboro.

On Thursday, we visited the temples in Prambanan. There are three big temples, the Brahmana, Syiwa and Wisnu temples. They are really amazing. We visited only Brahmana and Syiwa temples, because Wisnu temple is being renovated.

On Friday morning we went to Yogya Kraton. We spent about two hours there. We were lucky because we were led by a smart and friendly guide. Then we continued our journey to Borobudur. We arrived there at four p.m. At 5 p.m. we heard the announcement that Borobudur gate would be closed.

In the evening we left for Jakarta by wisata bus.

1. The text above mainly discusses about……..
a. the writer’s trip to Yogyakarta
b. the writer’s first visit to Prambanan
c. the writer’s impression about the guide
d. the writer’s experience at Yogya Kraton
e. the writer’s impression about Borobudur

Answer : A
