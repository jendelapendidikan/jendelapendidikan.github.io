18. tugas bahasa inggris impolite polite opinion disagreeng


Impolite
"I think your hair looks bad, it looks like you haven't take any bath for 2 weeks"

Polite
"I think your hair looks good, But maybe you should brush it a little"

Opinion
"My, your hairs are so beatiful. It's the most beatiful hair I ever see"

Disagree
"In my opinion, her hair look ridiculous. Haven't she wash it for the last 2 weeks?"

Contoh kalimat impolite

1. Can I borrow your ruler for a moment?

(Bisakah saya meminjam penggarismu sebentar?)

2. Will you please open the window?

(Maukah kamu menutup jendela?)

3. Will you accept my apology?

(Maukah kamu menerima permintaan maaf saya?)

4 Can you please answer the phone?

(Dapatkah kamu menjawab telepon?)

Contoh Kalimat Polite

1.Could I use your computer to print and scan?

(Dapatkah saya menggunakan komputermu untuk print dan scan?)

2. Would you like to see my handicraft?

(Maukah kamu melihat kerajinan tangan saya?)

3.Would you mind filling out this form?

(Maukah kamu mengisi formulir ini?)

4. Could you turn the volume down, please?

(Bisakah kamu mengecilkan volume?)

5. Could I leave class early today?

(Bisakah saya meninggalkan kelas lebih cepat hari ini?)

Contoh kalimat opinion dissagree

1. I think this movie is really incredible. (Saya pikir film ini benar-benar luar biasa.)

2. I don’t think so. (Saya tidak berpikir begitu.)

3. What do you think about that movie? (Apa pendapat kamu tentang film itu?)

4. I love that movie very much. (Saya sangat suka dengan film itu.)

5. I think you are wrong. I totally disagree with you. That movie was so boring. The plot is flat. It made me sleep when I watched it. (Saya pikir Anda salah. Saya benar-benar tidak setuju dengan Anda. Film yang sangat membosankan. Alurnya datar. Itu membuat saya tertidur ketika saya menontonnya.)
