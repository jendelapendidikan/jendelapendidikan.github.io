1. Jelaskan kelebihan dan kekurangan metode Focus Group Discussion! (minimal 3 poin) ​


Jawaban:

Direct Interaction

Focus Groups memungkinkan untuk secara langsung terlibat dengan pelanggan mereka. Mampu tatap muka dengan berbagai orang, demografi yang berbeda dapat memberikan wawasan yang lebih ‘nyata’ dan membantu untuk lebih mengenal pelanggan mereka.

Not just words or speech (in person only)

Dapat melihat peserta dan memonitor bahasa tubuh mereka dan tindakan lain dapat bermanfaat tergantung pada alasan di balik penelitian. Mereka juga akan dapat memberikan jawaban yang lebih mendalam dan lebih jelas daripada yang secara tertulis.

Time and Money Saving

Mewawancarai kelompok besar sekaligus lebih hemat biaya daripada mewawancarai secara individu. Anda tidak hanya menghemat waktu dengan mewawancarai beberapa orang sekaligus, tetapi Anda akan mendapat manfaat dari hanya memiliki satu sesi daripada beberapa sesi.Pengumpulan data lebih cepat.

22. Langkah langkah pembuatan paper small group discussion gimana yaa?​


Jawaban:

In order to create a small group discussion paper, you need to follow these steps:

Determine the topic for the discussion: Choose a topic that you and your group members are interested in and that you are willing to discuss together.

Assign roles to all the group members: Decide on the role each member will play during the discussion, such as the Chair, Scribe, Timekeeper, and Facilitator.

Organize and plan the discussion: Set the time, place, and duration of the discussion. Plan the activities to be carried out during the discussion, such as brainstorming, sharing opinions, and coming up with solutions.

Draft the discussion paper: Write down the ideas and solutions raised during the discussion, and list them in a clear and organized way. Make sure to summarize the key points of the discussion and to highlight the conclusions reached by the group.

Present the paper: Present the discussion paper in front of the class or the group members. This can be done in oral or written form, depending on the requirements set by the teacher or lecturer.

Evaluate the discussion paper: Evaluate the discussion paper based on the objective criteria set by the teacher or lecturer. This can include the clarity of the paper, the coherence of the ideas and solutions presented, and the overall impact of the discussion on the topic.

By following these steps, you will be able to create a small group discussion paper that is well-organized, structured, and effective in conveying the ideas and conclusions reached by the group.

27. In a group discussion, you will find several expressions uttered by membe the group. What kind of expression used for discussion? Mention it!​


Jawaban:

Here are some examples of expressions used in a group discussion:

1. **Opinions and Ideas:**

- "In my opinion, we should focus on implementing the new marketing strategy."

- "I believe the best approach is to increase our online presence."

2. **Agreement and Disagreement:**

- "I agree with John's suggestion to target younger audiences."

- "I respectfully disagree with Sarah's proposal; I think we should prioritize cost-cutting measures."

3. **Clarification and Explanation:**

- "Could you please clarify what you meant by 'market segmentation'?"

- "Let me explain the reasons behind our recent sales decline."

4. **Asking Questions:**

- "How do you think we can improve customer engagement?"

- "What data do we have to support this marketing campaign?"

5. **Summarizing:**

- "So, the main points we discussed are increasing social media ads and conducting market research."

- "In summary, we agree to launch the product in the second quarter."

6. **Supporting Evidence:**

- "Based on last year's sales data, targeting this demographic could lead to a significant increase in revenue."

- "Research shows that customer reviews play a crucial role in building brand credibility."

7. **Building on Ideas:**

- "I agree with Lisa's suggestion and would like to add that we could also collaborate with influencers for wider reach."

- "Expanding on John's point, we could consider offering loyalty rewards to retain existing customers."

8. **Active Listening:**

- "I understand your concern about the budget constraints, and we'll take that into consideration."

- "Thank you for sharing your insights; it gives us a new perspective on the issue."

9. **Suggesting Solutions:**

- "To address the delivery delays, we could explore partnering with a reliable courier service."

- "I propose conducting customer surveys to gather feedback on our new product line."

10. **Encouragement and Empathy:**

- "Great job on the presentation, Sarah! Your points were well-structured and persuasive."

Kakak akan menjelaskan lebih lanjut dalam pembahasan dibawah ini !^^

Pembahasan

Dalam pengumpulan data, terdapat data primer dan data sekunder.

Data primer adalah data yang diambil langsung dari narasumbernya. Pada soal terdapat pilihan ganda berupa survei, observasi, wawancara dan FGD (focus group discussion). Ke - empat metode penelitian ini merupakan metode penelitian yang memiliki data primer.

Data sekunder adalah data yang kita dapatkan dari pihak kedua maupun ketiga yang sudah tervalidasi benar. Dengan demikian jawaban yang tersisa yaitu studi pustaka, merupakan metode pengumpulan data sekunder.
