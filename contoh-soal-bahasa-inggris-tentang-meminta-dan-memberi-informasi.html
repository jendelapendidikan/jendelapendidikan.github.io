24. contoh meminta dan memberi informasi terkait sifat orang, binatang,benda, sesuai dengan kontek penggunaannya dalam bahasa Inggris


Asking for and Providing Information

Asking for information adalah kegiatan menanyakan suatu informasi. Providing information adalah memberi jawaban atau informasi atas pertanyaan dalam asking for information.

Untuk meminta informasi terkait sifat orang, binatang, atau benda, kita dapat menggunakan kalimat-kalimat seperti "What is it like", "What does she look like?".

Untuk memberi informasi tentang orang, binatang, dan benda, biasanya digunakan kata sifat (adjectives). Kata sifat yang digunakan untuk mendeskripsikan orang, binatang, atau benda, misalnya: handsome, kind, smart, diligent, small, thick, cute, shady, crowded, adorable, messy, dan lain-lain.

Berikut beberapa contoh kalimat asking for information dan providing information.

[Asking for information] ? [Providing Information]

What do the elephants look like? They are big.What does Mrs. Lia look like? She is tall, with long hair and a pointed nose.What is Fizzy like? She is smart and friendly.What are tigers like? They are strong and fierce.Why do you like a cat? It has soft fur and cute face.Why do you like your new house? It is big, with complete facilities.What does your cat look like? It has white, black, and brown fur.What is your mother like? She is good at cooking.What does the new student look like? She is tall and thin.
