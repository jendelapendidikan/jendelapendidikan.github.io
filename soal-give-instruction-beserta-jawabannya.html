7. tulisan 10 contoh soal pilihan ganda giving instruction beserta jawabannya ​


10 contoh soal Pilihan ganda dan jawabannya:

1. In which sentence is the instruction clear and easy to follow?

a. Please do the thing.

b. Turn the knob to the right until you hear a click.

c. The task should be completed.

d. Make sure to follow the procedure.

Jawaban: b. Turn the knob to the right until you hear a click.

2. What is the first step in assembling the furniture?

a. Read the instructions thoroughly.

b. Start with the biggest piece.

c. Gather all the tools.

d. Call for help.

Jawaban: a. Read the instructions thoroughly.

3. Which word is commonly used to begin an instruction?

a. Why

b. Therefore

c. First

d. Afterward

Jawaban: c. First

4. What should you do if you can't understand the instructions?

a. Ignore them.

b. Ask for clarification or help.

c. Give up.

d. Make your own instructions.

Jawaban: b. Ask for clarification or help.

5. Which of the following is NOT an example of a clear instruction?

a. "Add two cups of flour and mix well."

b. "Umm...do something with the ingredients."

c. "Stir the mixture gently for 3 minutes."

d. "Place the item in a preheated oven at 350°F."

Jawaban: b. "Umm...do something with the ingredients."

6. What is the best way to ensure someone follows your instructions?

a. Shout at them.

b. Write them in a language they don't understand.

c. Use clear and concise language.

d. Provide too many details.

Jawaban: c. Use clear and concise language.

7. What's the most common way to indicate a step in a list of instructions?

a. Using the word "again."

b. Numbering the steps.

c. Repeating the same instructions.

d. Using complex vocabulary.

Jawaban: b. Numbering the steps.

8. When providing instructions, it's important to:

a. Be vague and unclear.

b. Assume everyone knows what to do.

c. Use simple language and be specific.

d. Never provide any examples.

Jawaban: c. Use simple language and be specific.

9. What is the purpose of giving clear instructions?

a. To confuse people.

b. To make the task more difficult.

c. To ensure the task is completed successfully.

d. To waste time.

Jawaban: c. To ensure the task is completed successfully.

10. In an emergency situation, what's the most important thing to do when giving instructions?

a. Speak slowly and calmly.

b. Use complicated language to sound important.

c. Yell loudly.

d. Provide clear and concise directions.

Jawaban: a. Speak slowly and calmly.

21. buatlah dialogue tentang give instruction or ask for permission! ​


Penjelasan:

Dalam kehidupan sehari-hari, kita pasti tidak lepas dari yang namanya perizinan untuk melakukan sesuatu. Kita meminta izin atau permisi kepada orang lain sebagai bentuk penghormatan, juga sebagai bagian dari kesopanan.

Tidak jarang pula, orang lain yang meminta izin kepada kita untuk melakukan sesuatu.

Ketika ada yang meminta izin, kita tidak harus selalu mengabulkannya. Saat kita merasa keberatan, boleh saja kita menolak permintaannya. Akan tetapi, baiknya kita melakukan segala sesuatu dengan cara yang baik dan benar.

Maka dari itu, kali ini kita akan menambah pengetahuan tentang cara untuk meminta izin (asking for permission), memberi izin (giving permission), dan juga cara menolaknya (refusing permission) dalam bahasa Inggris.

Asking for Permission

1. Can I …?

Can I go with Mary tonight? (Bolehkah aku pergi dengan Mary malam ini)

Can I borrow your pen? (Bisakah kupinjam penamu?)

Can I try? (Bolehkah aku mencoba/mencicipi?)

Catatan:

Penggunaan “can” dalam permission sangat informal, bisa pula terkesan kurang sopan. Jadi, ada baiknya hanya digunakan dalam pembicaraan dengan orang-orang terdekat.

2. May I …?

May I have another cup of coffee? (Bolehkah saya minta secangkir kopi lagi)

May I see your daughter? (Bolehkah saya menemui anak perempuan anda?)

May I go to the bathroom? (Bolehkah saya pergi ke kamar mandi?)

Catatan:

Penggunaan “may” untuk saat ini terbilang lebih baku; baiknya digunakan dalam percakapan atau urusan formal.

3. Could I …?

Could I use the phone please? (Bisakah saya memakai teleponnya?)

Could I please hold the baby? (Bolehkah saya menimang bayinya?)

Could I eat this pizza? (Bolehkah saya makan pizza ini?)

4. Do you think I could …?

Do you think I could borrow the book? (Bolehkah saya meminjam bukunya?)

Do you think I could speak to my dad? (Bisakah saya berbicara dengan ayah saya?)

Do you think I could use your computer? (Bolehkah saya memakai komputermu?)

5. Would it be possible for me to …?

Would it be possible for me to study in this room? (Bolehkah saya belajar di ruangan ini?)

Would it be possible for me to meet Mr. Stephenson? (Bolehkah saya bertemu dengan Mr. Stephenson?)

Would it be possible for me to join your class? (Bolehkah saya ikut di kelas Anda?)

6. Would you mind if …?

Would you mind if I sit here? (Apakah Anda keberatan/bolehkah jika saya duduk di sini?)

Would you mind if I took a five minute break? (Apakah Anda keberatan jika saya beristirahat sebentar?)

Would you mind if I left now? (Apakah Anda keberatan jika saya pergi sekarang?)

atau bisa juga dengan menggunakan “Do you mind if I …?”

Do you mind if I sit here?

Do you mind if I leave now?

7. Is it okay if …?

Is it okay if I wait here? (Bolehkah saya menunggu di sini?)

Is it okay if we go now? (Bolehkah kami pergi sekarang?)

Is it okay if I wear this coat? (Bolehkah saya memakai mantel ini?)

Giving Permission

1. Yes, certainly (formal) (Ya, tentu)

2. Yes, please. (Ya, silahkan)

3. Yes, of course. (Ya, tentu saja)

4. Yes, you may do that. (Ya, kamu boleh melakukan)

5. Of course you can. (Tentu saja boleh)

6. No problem. (Tidak masalah/silahkan)

7. Please feel free. (Silahkan)

8. Sure (Tentu)

9. Go ahead (Silahkan)

10. No, I don’t mind (Saya tidak keberatan/silahkan)

Refusing Permission

1. Unfortunately, you can’t do that. (Sayangnya, kamu tidak boleh)

2. Sorry, I think it’s impossible for you. (Maaf, saya rasa tidak bisa)

3. I have to say no for this case. (Saya harus tidak mengijinkan kali ini)

4. You cannot do that. (Kamu tidak diperbolehkan)

5. You could not do that. (Anda tidak diperbolehkan)

6. I am afraid not. (Maaf, tidak bisa)

7. No, you may not. (Tidak, Anda tidak diperbolehkan)

8. Sorry, you are not permitted. (Maaf, Anda tidak diperbolehkan)

Agar lebih jelas, coba simak contoh dialog berikut.

Dialogue I

Anna: Hi Dad, today is Laura Birthday. Tonight there will be a party at her house. (Halo pak, hari ini Laura ulang tahun. Akan ada pesta ulang tahun di rumahnya)

Dad: And then? (Lalu?)

Anna: May I go to Laura’s Birthday Party? (Bolehkan aku pergi ke pesta ulang tahunnya?)

Dad: At what time you will get home? (Jam berapa kamu akan pulang?)

Anna: Before 11, I promise. (Sebelum jam 11, aku janji)

Dad: Who will pick you up? (Siapa yang menjemputmu?)

Anna: James. (James)

Dad: I have to say no. Unless me who take you there. (Tidak boleh. Kecuali aku yang akan mengantarmu)

Anna: OK dad. I will be ready at 7. (OK pak. Aku akan siap jam 7)

Dialogue II

James: John, sorry I forgot to bring my pen. May I borrow your pen? (John, maaf saya lupa membawa pena. Bolehkan aku meminjam penamu)

John: Sure. I brought three pens. Here you are. (Baiklah. Aku bawa tiga pena. Ini..)

James: Thanks John. Is it OK if I borrow your ruler too? (Terima kasih John. Apakah boleh pinjam penggarisnya juga?)

John: Yes, of course. just use it. (Iya tentunya, pakai saja)

James: Thank you so much. (Terima kasih banyak)

John: No problem. (Tidak masalah)

30. To give instruction we say.... ​


Giving Instructions

The examples:

Wash your hand regularly and thoroughly.

Do the task by following the instruction below.

Submit your assignment on time.

Brush the pan with cooking oil.

Heat a steamer pan.

The translation:

Cuci tangan mu secara teratur dan menyeluruh.

Kerjakan tugas dengan mengikuti instruksi di bawah ini.

Kirimkan tugas mu tepat waktu.

Olesi wajan dengan minyak goreng.

Panaskan panci kukusan.

PEMBAHASAN:

Giving instruction is an expression that used to give or ask somebody to do something that what we want.

Ada dua bentuk pemberian instruksi, yaitu:

1. Verbal instruction

Rumus

Verb 1 + object!

Contoh:

Open your book.

Read the text and answer the questions.

2. Nominal instruction

Rumus

Be + adjective/noun/adverb !

Contoh :

Be careful.

Be brave.
